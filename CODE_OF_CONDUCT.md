# Code of conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone. Be respectful, assume good
intent while evaluating technical claims on evidence, accept constructive feedback, and avoid
personal attacks or discriminatory language.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently restrict participation
for abusive, threatening, harassing, or disruptive behavior. Report conduct concerns privately to
the repository maintainers. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.

This policy applies in project spaces and when representing the project in public spaces.
